Frequently Asked Questions About STUDYPALS



How to create a group session. To create a group session with your PALS click on "Start Meeting" and invite your friends to study together. This session can be recorded for later use and it includes features like raise hand, chat, number of participents shown, list of participents with there names, white board, screen sharing, audio and video.
For any further questions contact StudyPALS.

How to Schedule a Session. To schedule a session on your phones and laptop all you have to do is click on "Open Calendar" and here you can schedule a session of your choice. An alert notification will be sent on your phones/laptop before the schedule time so that you can prepare for your session.
For any further queries contact StudyPALS.

how To Ask Queries. To ask your difficult and confusing questions all you have to do is click on "Open AI Bot" and then you can ask anything you want. This AI Bot will give perfect solutions to your problems and will help to make your studies more easy and fun.
For any further queries ask StudyPALS.

How to Use a Chat Group. To chat with your PALS and studymates click on "Chat App". Here you can chat with all your studymates anytime. Send messages can also be deleted right after sending them click on "Delete" and your message will be deleted.
For further queris ask StudyPALS.
